This tone clip is a moving look book for the series, The Beautiful Future, intended to establish the visual style and mood.

The Beautiful Future is a science prediction narrative TV series about a young woman crippled by her mostly digital existence. She falls in love with an offline advocate and he incites an awakening in her, amidst a society battling between techno-optimism and a return to the organic. Comprised of eight scripted episodes, it covers topics like privacy, automation, brain-computer interfaces, digital citizenship, machine intelligence, and entropy.
