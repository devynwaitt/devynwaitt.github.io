This tone clip is a moving look book for the series, The Beautiful Future, intended to establish the visual style and mood.

The Beautiful Future is a science prediction limited TV series. 8 scripted episodes, covering topics like regenerative medicine, net neutrality, automation, augmented reality, and of course, love.

Logline: In the years preceding an intelligence explosion, futurist themes are explored through an introverted dreamer, drawn out of her shell by a boy with blue hair. Over the span of a decade, they chase each other back and forth through a world of digital natives.
