Challenger

Challenger is a band that I played in from 2010-2013. In addition to playing and writing music, I designed the live experience of the shows, created the album art, and directed several music videos.

The People in the Aquifer

The area of Oldsmar that I live in is currently being developed by Ryland Homes, and the community is angry. In response, I have co-written a conservationist horror movie, with the developer as the villain, exploring the ecological crisis of a crumbling aquifer, and showcasing the disappearing beauty of the Mobbly Bayou. A short version of the film was commissioned by Borscht Corp., Miami for inclusion in Borscht Diez.
