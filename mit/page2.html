The Beautiful Future

The Beautiful Future is a science prediction narrative TV series. Limited, it consists of eight scripted episodes, covering topics like privacy, automation, brain-computer interfaces, digital citizenship, machine intelligence, and entropy.

Logline: Ava, a young woman crippled by her mostly digital existence, falls in love with newcomer Francis, who incites an offline awakening in her amidst a society battling between techno-optimism and a return to the organic.

Tone Clip

This ‘tone clip’ is a moving look book for the series, intended to establish the visual style and mood.

It is my intention to use tropes, and classic storytelling with pop sensibility, to communicate critical theory and critical design to a mainstream audience. “A spoonful of sugar,” is the way I have always thought about the commercial aspects of the series, and their role as a vehicle for grander ideas and criticisms about our relationship with technology.
